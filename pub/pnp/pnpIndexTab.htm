Policies vs. Procedures
What's the difference?

Simply put, Policies are a guide on "what" to do, while Procedures are a guide on "how" to do it.

Polices define the rules and guidelines on how the company & it's agents conduct business. These help protect the agent and company from liability, and ensure we are following applicable laws to serve our clients.

Procedures, explain how to perform certain tasks and create a repeatable guide to an end result, so everyone is rowing in the same direction to achieve a common goal.

Policies and Procedures (PnP) are a living documents that evolve as the need arises. When you have a question about PnP, always check here first for the most up to date information!
